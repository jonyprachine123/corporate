Our Story – From a Dream to a Movement

In 2022, an inspiring conversation over a cup of tea with Belayet Bhai sparked the beginning of our journey. What started as a simple idea blossomed into Prachin Bangla Limited—a noble mission dedicated to making life easier, advancing society, and tirelessly fostering new entrepreneurs.

We firmly believe—

"An entrepreneur is one who dreams and brings those dreams to life."
"Initiative is the most powerful tool for transforming society."

Our work is more than business; it is a commitment to creating positive change in people's lives. By delivering medicine to every home in Chattogram, establishing modern hospitals and diagnostic centers, building care homes, and empowering the youth as entrepreneurs, we strive to build a healthier, aware, and self-reliant community.

We understand that change begins with a courageous step. Turning dreams into reality demands unwavering determination, hard work, and steadfast perseverance. And that is the path we walk—
