About Prachin Bangla Limited

Corporate Snapshot Prachin Bangla Limited is a forward-looking enterprise built for large-scale growth in Bangladesh.It blends a modern multivendor e-commerce platform with a strong commitment to Bengali heritage, health, and social development positioning itself as a high-potential destination for international investment.

Core Mandate
Prachin Bangla operates a secure, inclusive platform empowering local entrepreneurs. It features a curated portfolio of indigenous herbal, natural, and handicraft products, while strategically integrating commercial success with public service initiatives in healthcare, education, and research.
