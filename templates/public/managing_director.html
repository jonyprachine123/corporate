Managing Director

MD Belayet Hossain brings exceptional leadership and strategic vision to Prachin Bangla Limited. Under his guidance, the company has grown to become a symbol of quality, integrity, and innovation in Bangladesh's business landscape.

His leadership philosophy centers on the belief that true success cannot be measured solely by financial results, but by the positive changes a company brings to people's lives. This principle forms the foundation of Prachin Bangla's journey.

With a vision to harmoniously blend Bangladesh's heritage, culture, economy, and modern technology, he leads the company towards building a sustainable future while maintaining the highest standards of honesty, humanity, and long-term planning.

Managing Director's Message

Dear Readers, Partners, and Well-wishers,

On behalf of Prachin Bangla Limited, I extend my heartfelt greetings, gratitude, and love to you all.

We believe that the true success of a company cannot be measured solely by financial results; rather, it is defined by the positive changes it brings to people's lives. This belief is the foundation of Prachin Bangla's journey.

Our vision is not only to build a strong business organization but also to create a sustainable future where Bangladesh's heritage, culture, economy, and modern technology come together in harmony. In every product, service, and decision, we strive to reflect quality, integrity, and innovation.

We recognize that competition in today's world is intense. Yet, we firmly believe that honesty, humanity, and long-term planning are the real strengths to overcome any challenge. Our business principles always prioritize the interests of our customers, partners, investors, and the society we serve.

Our greatest achievement in this journey has been your trust. Your love and support have inspired us to move forward and given us the courage to dream even bigger for the future.

I strongly believe that Prachin Bangla Limited is not just a company, but a promise—where every initiative carries a story of development, and every success bears your contribution. Let us work together to build a Bangladesh where, rooted in tradition and embracing modernity, we all move forward towards a brighter future.

Wishing you all the very best.

MD BELAYET HOSSAIN

Managing Director
