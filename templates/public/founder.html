Our Founder

Meet the visionary leader who founded our organization with a mission to create positive social impact

Belayet Hossain

Founder & Managing Director

Founder's Biography

Belayet Hossain is a visionary entrepreneur and a man of action whose journey began at the grassroots level of Bangladesh. Rising through personal determination and deep understanding of the nation's social and economic landscape, he has dedicated his life to blending business excellence with social responsibility.

Driven by the belief that Bangladesh's youth can become the engine of national progress if provided with the right opportunities, he established Prachin Bangla Limited in 2022. His leadership philosophy is rooted in service, innovation, and ethical growth — ensuring that every business initiative contributes to the betterment of society.

Company Story

Prachin Bangla Limited is more than a company — it is a movement to build a healthy, prosperous, and ethical society. Founded in Chattogram, Bangladesh, the organization operates in multiple sectors, including health products, hospitals, foundations, social development, and entrepreneurship training.

The company's philosophy is to combine the rich heritage of Bengal with modern technology to deliver sustainable and impactful solutions. Under Belayet Hossain's leadership, Prachin Bangla Limited focuses equally on economic growth and social responsibility, creating an ecosystem where business success benefits the wider community.

Mission, Vision & Values

Mission

"To build a healthy, prosperous, and ethical society by providing quality products and services for the health, education, and economic development of the people."

Key mission priorities include:

  • • Supplying safe, affordable health products
  • • Offering world-class hospital services
  • • Promoting health awareness nationwide
  • • Fostering entrepreneurship and skill development

Vision

"To become a leading ethical and innovative business group in Bangladesh, bringing the light of health, education, and development to every individual in the country."

The long-term vision focuses on:

  • • Raising healthcare standards
  • • Leveraging innovative technologies
  • • Creating sustainable employment
  • • Preserving environmental and social balance
