Our Founder

Meet the visionary leader who founded our organization with a mission to create positive social impact

Belayet Hossain

Founder & Managing Director

Belayet Hossain, a visionary entrepreneur, founded "Prachin Bangla Limited" in 2022 in Chattogram, Bangladesh. This organization is committed to improving the quality of life for the people of Bangladesh through its initiatives in health products, hospitals, a foundation, and social development programs.

Belayet Hossain is both a visionary and a man of action. Rising from the grassroots level, he possesses a profound understanding of Bangladesh's social and economic landscape. He firmly believes that with the right opportunities and guidance, the nation's youth can become the primary driver of development.

Under his leadership, Prachin Bangla Limited is more than just a commercial entity; it is a social movement aimed at serving the country and its people. The company's core objective is to build a healthy and prosperous society by blending the rich heritage of Bengal with modern technology and innovative ideas.
