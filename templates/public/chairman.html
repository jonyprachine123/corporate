MD Muslim Uddin Chowdhury

Chairman

From the Seed of Dreams to the Promise of Ancient Bengal. There are some dreams on the path of life, which are born not only for us but for the entire society. I have also had such a dream in my mind for a long time—where business will be a tool of service, and profit will be a gift spent for the welfare of people.

Today, that seed has sprouted and stands on firm roots, providing shade for everyone. On our journey are— E-commerce, which is delivering the country's products to the people and outside the country. Hospitals and diagnostics, which provide quality and accessible healthcare. Resorts, where people find a touch of peace and tranquility. Old age homes, which are safe havens of love and dignity for people at the end of their lives. And many more social initiatives are coming up—which aim to make people’s lives better.

I believe that real progress is not possible without the development of society. The true measure of business success is how many people we are able to bring smiles to.

Our Story – From a Dream to a Movement

At one time in 2022, some inspiring words heard from Belayet Bhai and Mizanur Rahman Rubel lit a new light in my heart. I felt then—my long-awaited destination had been found. At that moment, the seed of a dream was silently planted—named "Prachin Bangla Limited"
